Practice Areas

We are highly skilled and experienced in these areas

Civil Litigation

Civil litigation is the process of resolving a legal dispute between two or more parties (individuals or business entities) who seek compensation (in the form of money) for damages incurred or specific performances that were not delivered. It encompasses not just the representations made in Court but also the pre-trial procedures including interlocutory hearings, and the port-trial procedures such as costs and enforcement of a judgment.

Intellectual Property

We deal in matters concerning Intellectual Property Rights and other related Intellectual Property Rights such as agency and labour related matters as may be required by our clients

Conveyancing

Conveyance is the act of transferring an ownership interest in property from one party to another. Conveyance also refers to the written instrument, such as a deed or lease, that transfers legal title of a property from the seller to the buyer.

Criminal Litigation

Criminal Litigation is the process of going to trial in a criminal court to either prosecute or defend oneself in a criminal matter. Criminal litigators come in two varieties: criminal prosecutors, who present the government's case against the defendant, and criminal defense attorneys, who represent the interests of the defendant.
